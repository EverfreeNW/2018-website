Everfree Northwest 2018 Charity Donations

Everfree Northwest embraces the Elements of Harmony and the Magic of Friendship they represent. Our amazing staff volunteer their time and talents and encourage everyone to join them in expressing the Element of Generosity.

Our annual charity auction at Everfree Northwest is a cherished event. Items from amazingly generous people are donated to Pegasi Northwest, a federal 501(c)(3) corporation, which are then auctioned to the highest bidder, further increasing their value and lasting impact on our community. The proceeds from the auction, combined with other fundraising efforts during the event and throughout the year, benefit our carefully selected annual charity. To date, our attendees and staff have helped raise over $130,000 to benefit local and national charity organizations.

If you are interested in donating an item for our charity auction, please read the following letter for prospective donors, and fill out the intent to donate form.

Letter from our Charity Lead to Prospective Donors

Thank you for your interest in providing a donation for the Everfree Northwest 2018 Charity Auction. Before making your donation, I'd like to share some additional information with you regarding the format of the Charity Auction for 2018.

This year we will still be hosting a Silent Auction prior to the Live Auction. Items with high or active bidding will be moved to the Live Auction. We appreciate all donated items, but with limited time during the Live Auction, we are unable to guarantee that any particular item will make it to the Live Auction.

Please be aware that your donated item may be grouped with other items on one bidding ticket. This is to help streamline the bidding process and limit the individual bidding numbers. We also find that grouping similar items or combining items that share a common theme tend to increase the value of those items.

This year we are proud to announce that charity donations will benefit Washington Autism Alliance & Advocacy.

Finally, we would like to give credit to where credit is due. If you wish to be recognized for your donation, please indicate that we have your permission to credit you during the Silent Auction and/or Live Auction for your generosity and kindness.

Thank you again for your interest in contributing to the Everfree Northwest 2018 Charity Auction. Please feel free to contact us by email with any additional questions.

Sincerely,
Everfree Northwest 2018 Staff
charity@everfreenw.com
