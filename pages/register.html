The Fine Print

Teen and foal badge discounts apply to the attendees age at the time of the convention. Attendees age 12 and under must be accompanied at all times by adult of age 18 or older with a paid badge. All badge registrations are non-refundable. If you have any questions about badge types or registration, please email registration@everfreenw.com.
