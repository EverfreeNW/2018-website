Ready to show off your artistic talent and creativity? Join the Everfree Northwest 2018 art contest and compete for awesome prizes and community recognition! There will be one grand prize and 6 specially themed placements:

  • Grand Prize: The best overall!
    Receives a plush voucher, con store voucher, con water bottle and a free critique from the design staff (optional)!
     
  • Rainbow Dash: The most jaw dropping
  • Applejack: The most true to the show
  • Rarity: The most elegant
  • Pinkie Pie: The most fun
  • Fluttershy: The sweetest/cutest
  • Twilight: The most well thought-out
    Mane 6 placements each get a con store voucher and button!

All entries will be featured on the con website for all attendees to see.

Rules:
Your artwork must include at least one of the Everfree Northwest mascots (Front Page, Mocha, Marina, Novella and/or Sharpie). Images must be family friendly, but other than that the subject is open!

Please ensure your entry fits the following requirements:
Size: 8.5 inch x 11 inch (letter size, any orientation)
Resolution: At least 150 dpi

Entries are due by April 4th at 11:59 PM PST
